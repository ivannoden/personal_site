IMA TMT 2024 Conference: Patterns in Prime Numbers

The slides for a group presentation for a general audience on the topic Patterns in Prime Numbers centering around a discussion of Bunyakovsky’s Conjecture, in which we touch on Dirichlet’s Theorem, Schinzel’s Conjecture, the Twin Prime Conjecture and more. Last summer, I, Bruno Eaves, and Ioannis Pantelidakis gave a presentation on Patterns in Prime Numbers, focusing on a discussion around Bunyakovsky’s conjecture which captures many open questions related to prime numbers. This March, we were very grateful to have the opportunity to give this presentation at the IMA’s ‘Tomorrow’s Mathematicians Today 2024’ undergraduate conference. It was a wonderful event with many great talks so I would very much like to thank the IMA, the University of Greenwich, Tony Mann, and all others involved in organising the conference which you can read more about here.
